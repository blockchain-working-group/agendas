Sandbox

Technologies and platforms that have come up in working group discussions. Links are provided for exploration—inclusion here is not an endorsement.

Ethereum

Programmable blockchain with smart contract support. Home of the EVM ecosystem, widely used for DeFi, identity, and tokenization.
F1R3FLY.io

Decentralized compute and storage platform built on Rholang and RChain technology.
Bitcoin (BTC)

The original blockchain network. Proof-of-work consensus, focused on peer-to-peer value transfer and digital scarcity.
Bitcoin Cash (BCH)

A Bitcoin fork with larger block sizes, targeting faster and cheaper on-chain transactions.
Bitcoin SV (BSV)

A Bitcoin Cash fork emphasizing massive scaling and enterprise data use cases on-chain.
Hyperledger

Linux Foundation umbrella for enterprise-grade permissioned blockchain frameworks including Fabric, Besu, and Iroha.
SIMBA Chain

Blockchain-as-a-service platform with a focus on government and defense supply chain applications.

Demo Projects

Open-source demos and reference implementations from working group members and the broader community.
